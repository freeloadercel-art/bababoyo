SPATIAL ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK

Spatial Hypotheses

This project uses geographic information systems to test whether the Holodomor was a deliberate, ethnically targeted genocide. The following four hypotheses are examined through interactive maps and statistical analysis.

H1: Agricultural Targeting

Mortality highest in black‑soil regions (Poltava, Cherkasy) where grain quotas were extreme.

H2: Ethnic Targeting

Areas with higher ethnic Ukrainian population suffered greater losses, even controlling for agriculture.

H3: Industrial Protection

Industrial Donbas showed lower mortality due to state rations for workers.

H4: Blacklist Clustering

Villages on Soviet "Black Lists" cluster in highest‑mortality zones.
